Privacy Policy — NarVara BrainGame

Last updated: 10 June 2026

NarVara Development ("we", "us") operates the NarVara BrainGame mobile application (the "App"). This page explains what data we collect, why, and your choices. By using the App you agree to this policy.

Contact: narvaradevelopment@gmail.com

1. Data We Collect

a. Account information (only if you sign in) — Email address and display name when you register or sign in with Google. Playing as Guest or without login collects no account information.

b. Gameplay data — Scores, high scores, streaks, games played, and difficulty progress, used to power the leaderboard and your personal stats.

c. Purchase data — When you buy Premium (ad removal), the transaction is processed by Google Play Billing. We receive a purchase token to verify premium status. We never receive or store your card/payment details.

d. Advertising data — The App shows ads via Google AdMob, which may collect a device advertising identifier and usage signals to serve and measure ads (details). Premium users see no ads.

e. Device & technical data — Standard diagnostic data (app version, OS version, crash logs) to keep the App working.

f. AI-generated content — Some puzzles are generated using the Groq API. Only the puzzle prompt is sent — no personal data is included.

2. How We Use Data

Provide and improve gameplay, leaderboard, and stats; verify premium purchases and remove ads; show and measure advertising (non-premium users); diagnose crashes. We do not sell your personal data.

3. Where Data Is Stored

Account and gameplay data are stored securely on Supabase (PostgreSQL, Asia-Pacific region). Local data (settings, language, cached progress) is stored on your device.

4. Third-Party Services

  • Google AdMob — policies.google.com/privacy
  • Google Play Billing — policies.google.com/privacy
  • Supabase — supabase.com/privacy
  • Groq — groq.com/privacy-policy

5. Data Retention & Deletion

Gameplay/account data is kept while your account is active. To delete your account and data, email narvaradevelopment@gmail.com with the subject "Delete my account"; we will remove your data within 30 days. Guest/no-login data exists only on your device and is removed when you uninstall the App.

6. Children's Privacy

The App is rated for general audiences. It is not directed at children under 13, and we do not knowingly collect personal data from them.

7. Your Rights

You may request access to, correction of, or deletion of your personal data by emailing narvaradevelopment@gmail.com.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy. Changes will be posted on this page with a new "Last updated" date.

9. Contact

NarVara Development — narvaradevelopment@gmail.com

Kebijakan Privasi — NarVara BrainGame

Diperbarui: 10 Juni 2026

NarVara Development ("kami") mengoperasikan aplikasi NarVara BrainGame ("Aplikasi"). Halaman ini menjelaskan data apa yang kami kumpulkan, alasannya, dan pilihan Anda.

Kontak: narvaradevelopment@gmail.com

1. Data yang Kami Kumpulkan

  • Informasi akun (hanya jika login): email & nama tampilan saat daftar atau login via Google. Mode Tamu / tanpa login tidak mengumpulkan data akun.
  • Data permainan: skor, rekor, streak, jumlah main, progres kesulitan — untuk leaderboard & statistik.
  • Data pembelian: pembelian Premium (hapus iklan) diproses Google Play Billing. Kami tidak pernah menerima/menyimpan data kartu Anda.
  • Data iklan: iklan via Google AdMob (memakai ID iklan perangkat). Pengguna Premium tidak melihat iklan.
  • Data teknis perangkat: versi app/OS, log crash.
  • Konten AI: sebagian soal dibuat via Groq API, hanya prompt soal yang dikirim — tanpa data pribadi.

2. Penggunaan Data

Menjalankan permainan, leaderboard, statistik; verifikasi Premium; menampilkan & mengukur iklan; memperbaiki kestabilan. Kami tidak menjual data pribadi Anda.

3. Penyimpanan

Data akun & permainan disimpan aman di Supabase (region Asia-Pasifik). Data lokal disimpan di perangkat Anda.

4. Layanan Pihak Ketiga

Google AdMob, Google Play Billing, Supabase, Groq — masing-masing punya kebijakan privasi sendiri (lihat tautan di versi Inggris di atas).

5. Penyimpanan & Penghapusan Data

Untuk menghapus akun & data, email narvaradevelopment@gmail.com subjek "Hapus akun saya". Kami hapus dalam 30 hari. Data mode Tamu hanya ada di perangkat & terhapus saat uninstall.

6. Privasi Anak

7. Hak Anda

Anda dapat meminta akses, koreksi, atau penghapusan data via email di atas.

8. Perubahan
